# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 21, 2025
**Effective Date:** December 21, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to AI Product Photography Bot. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- Telegram profile data (user ID, username, name)
- Product photos you upload for processing
- Support inquiries and feedback

### Automatically Collected
- Usage data (generation history, timestamps)
- Telegram language preference for localization
- UTM parameters from marketing links

### Payment Information
- Transaction IDs and amounts
- Purchase timestamps

**Note:** We DO NOT store credit card numbers. All payment processing is handled securely by YooKassa.

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

1. **Service Delivery** - Process images using AI, manage your account
2. **Customer Support** - Respond to inquiries, resolve issues
3. **Service Improvement** - Analyze usage, improve AI quality
4. **Marketing** - Track referral program, send promotional offers (with consent)
5. **Legal Compliance** - Prevent fraud, enforce Terms of Service

## Third-Party Services

We share data with:

- **OpenRouter/Anthropic Claude** - For prompt generation
- **Google Gemini** - For image generation
- **YooKassa** - Payment processing
- **Telegram Analytics** - Usage analytics

Privacy Policies:
- Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
- Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- YooKassa: https://yookassa.ru/docs/support/payments/

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: While account is active
- **Transaction History**: 7 years (tax compliance)
- **Generated Images**: 30 days, then deleted
- **Support Tickets**: 1 year after resolution

## Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Request data in machine-readable format
- Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise your rights, use /support command in the bot.

## Data Security

We implement:
- Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL)
- Secure database storage
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits

## Children's Privacy

Our bot is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
